International Logistics

Running a booth in another country.

  • Freight Booth structure, product, and display materials shipped internationally and cleared for the show.
  • Build On-site stand assembly coordinated with show contractors on a fixed move-in window.
  • Branding Signage, graphics, and three-brand layout produced ahead of time and installed on location.
  • Staffing On-floor schedule and demo plan organized for a small traveling team.
  • Dealer event Off-floor venue, invitations, and hosting arranged in-city for partners and prospects.
The Dealer Event

A reason to show up, and stay.

Beyond the stand, an off-floor dealer event gave existing and prospective partners a reason to gather, talk, and build the relationships that a booth alone can't. Planned, organized, and hosted alongside everything else, in a city none of us were based in.
